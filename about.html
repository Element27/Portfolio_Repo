About Me

Akinbo Olamilekan Daniel is a frontend web developer and a fellow at the TIIDELab cohort 4 fellowship where he is currently sharpening his technical, problem solving and communication skills. He is knowledgeable with frontend stacks such as html, css, basic javascript, react and nodejs, and is familiar with Java, all of which are relevant to the end goal of being a full stack programmer/developer.

Olamilekan holds his first degree in Mass Communication from Yaba College of Technology and hopes to inspire individuals of all ages and demography irrespective of educational background to pursue a career in tech.

In his spare time, Olamilekan plays around with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and Corel Draw. studies the Bible,follows Real Madrid and listens to music.
